Building a resilient base is the only way to reach the 100-day milestone. A disorganized raft will lead to cluttered movement and easy destruction during boss encounters. Follow this structural roadmap to ensure your survival.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish the Core">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Vertical Expansion">
