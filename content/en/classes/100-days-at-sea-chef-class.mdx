<Section icon={Sparkles} title="100 Days At Sea Chef Class: Role and Overview">

Surviving the Bermuda Triangle in Roblox requires more than just sharp weapons and a sturdy raft; it demands a sustainable food supply. The **100 days at sea chef class** playstyle represents the ultimate setup for players who want to conquer the 100-day milestone by mastering the cooking pot, hunger management, and team support. 

In this open-world ocean survival game, hunger is a silent killer. If your hunger bar drops to zero, your health begins to decay rapidly, making you vulnerable to shark attacks, night raids, and boss encounters. While offensive classes like the Swordsman or Raider focus on combat, a dedicated chef ensures that the entire crew remains buffed, healed, and well-fed throughout the run.

| Item | Type | Hunger Restored | Additional Buffs |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Raw Fish** | Ingredient | Low | None, risk of minor food poisoning |
| **Kelp** | Ingredient | Very Low | Minor hydration recovery |
| **Chowder** | Cooked Meal | High | 10% faster regeneration, extra oxygen |
| **Egg Bomb** | Utility | None | Explodes on impact, clears small groups |
| **Luxury Meal** | Cooked Meal | Maximum | Full health recovery, stamina boost |

By focusing on the culinary aspect of survival, you transform basic, low-yield raw ingredients into powerful cooked meals. This transition is essential for long-term survival, especially when pushing past day 50 and heading toward the day 100 milestone.

<Callout type="success" title="Survival Tip">
Eating cooked meals like Chowder prevents the health decay associated with eating raw fish or kelp, saving your healing supplies for combat.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="How to Unlock and Equip the Chef Class">

While there is no single button labeled "Chef" in the starter menu, players achieve the chef playstyle by unlocking and equipping specific food-centric classes. The most effective class for this role is the **Survivor** class, which costs 50 Pearls and starts with a pre-crafted Chowder. The Survivor class grants passive buffs that reduce hunger decay and increase health regeneration, making it the perfect foundation for your culinary build.

To fund your class unlocks and companion chests early in the run, you should take advantage of active developer codes. For instance, players can redeem the active code **20Pearls** via the in-game Codes tab to get a head start on their build.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Redeem Active Codes">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlock the Survivor Class">

  </Step>

<Section icon={Target} title="Cooking Pot Mechanics and Recipes">

The Cooking Pot is the central workstation for any aspiring chef. To maximize your cooking efficiency, you must understand the cooking times and ingredient combinations required for each recipe. Preparing food in advance is critical before embarking on long voyages to mysterious islands or initiating boss fights like the Squid Boss.

As you upgrade your raft's bonfire, you will unlock better environmental safety, making it easier to cook during stormy weather and night cycles. Below is the primary recipe book used by top-tier chefs to sustain their runs.

| Recipe Name | Required Ingredients | Cooking Time | Best Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic Chowder** | 2x Raw Fish, 1x Kelp | 15 Seconds | General survival and daily hunger management |
| **Hearty Stew** | 3x Raw Fish, 1x Clean Water | 20 Seconds | Pre-boss fight health buffer |
| **Island Salad** | 2x Wild Berries, 1x Kelp | 10 Seconds | Quick stamina recovery during exploration |
| **Seafood Feast** | 4x Rare Fish, 2x Kelp | 30 Seconds | Co-op team recovery before major raids |

Mastering these recipes allows you to support your team during high-difficulty runs. Keeping a steady supply of Chowder in your inventory ensures that your health regeneration remains active, allowing you to save your Medkits for emergencies.

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Chef Setup">
Place a storage chest directly next to your Cooking Pot. Label it or dedicate it solely to raw ingredients so you can cook rapidly without searching your inventory.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Best Class Synergies and Companion Builds">

For example, the **Crow** (obtained from the Magma Chest) and the **Seagull** (obtained from the Pirate Chest) both search the surroundings and bring food to the player every 120 seconds. This passive income of raw ingredients ensures your Cooking Pot is always active.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Raft Setup for Optimal Cooking">

A chef is only as good as their kitchen. When building your raft base, you must design a safe, centralized layout that protects your cooking station, bonfire, and storage systems from hostile raids and environmental hazards. 

As you progress through your 100-day journey, upgrading your bonfire from level 1 to level 9 should be your primary structural goal. A higher-level bonfire provides better light, warmth, and protection, which directly correlates with safer cooking conditions during the dangerous night cycles.

| Structure | Priority | Material Cost | Key Benefit for Chefs |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Cooking Pot** | **High** | Basic Materials | Unlocks the ability to craft Chowder and Stews |
| **Bonfire Level 5** | **Medium** | Moderate Materials | Increases overall raft safety and light radius |
| **Storage Chests** | **High** | Low Materials | Keeps raw ingredients organized and safe from despawning |
| **Raft Defenses** | **Maximum** | High Materials | Protects the central cooking bay from shark attacks |

By prioritizing these structures, you build a resilient base of operations. This setup is particularly crucial when defending against late-game threats like the Ghost Galleon or during high-tier regional exploration in the Ice Biome and Volcano zones.
