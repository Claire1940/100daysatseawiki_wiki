<Section icon={Swords} title="Fisherman Trading & Progression Mechanics">

Once liberated, the fisherman becomes a vital trading partner who can fast-track your gear progression [svg4uQ6ZHYI]. To initiate trading, you must complete one mandatory trade [svg4uQ6ZHYI]. This requires you to catch a specific fish using your fishing rod [svg4uQ6ZHYI]. The trading interface can be slightly finicky: you must equip the fish so that a selection outline appears around the item in your inventory, and then drag it directly into the fisherman's trading zone [svg4uQ6ZHYI]. Dropping the fish without the active selection outline will cause it to fall to the ground [svg4uQ6ZHYI].

<Callout type="tip" title="Trading Tip">
Make sure the fish has a clear selection outline before dragging. If you drop it in the air without the outline active, the item might drop on the ground or fail to register [svg4uQ6ZHYI].
</Callout>

Completing the mandatory trade unlocks a series of highly rewarding optional trades [svg4uQ6ZHYI]. These trades allow you to exchange rare catches and crafted items for high-tier gear that cannot be easily obtained elsewhere [svg4uQ6ZHYI]. By targeting these specific trade milestones, you can rapidly upgrade your defensive and offensive capabilities [svg4uQ6ZHYI].

| Unlockable Item | Required Materials | Utility & Stats | Priority |
| Machete | Crab Trap | High melee damage, replaces basic knife | High |
| 10-Slot Good Sack | Sushi | Doubles inventory storage capacity | High |
| Flintlock Pistol | Jar of Jelly, Flintlock Ammo | Ranged weapon, high single-target damage | Medium |

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Crafting & Resource Management Priority">

Survival in the open ocean demands efficient resource management [svg4uQ6ZHYI]. The three primary resources you must manage are Wood Scraps, Metal Scraps, and Ghost Goo [svg4uQ6ZHYI]. While it is tempting to craft a cooking spot or a crab trap immediately, your absolute highest priority should be building a Sawmill [svg4uQ6ZHYI]. The Sawmill automates your wood production, generating wooden planks passively so you do not have to spend hours manually harvesting floating debris with your harpoon [svg4uQ6ZHYI].

<Callout type="success" title="Automation Success">
Crafting the Sawmill early eliminates the tedious grind of manual wood harvesting. It generates wood scraps passively, allowing you to focus entirely on exploration [svg4uQ6ZHYI].
</Callout>

Once your wood production is automated, you can pivot your resources toward crafting advanced transport, such as the Dinghy [svg4uQ6ZHYI]. The Dinghy offers unmatched speed, making trips between your home base and the red-roofed island incredibly swift [svg4uQ6ZHYI]. However, the Dinghy requires fuel, which must be systematically gathered from chests scattered across the ocean's minor islands [svg4uQ6ZHYI].

| Resource Type | Primary Source | Crafting Utility | Gathering Difficulty |
| Wood Scraps | Floating debris, Sawmill | Raft upgrades, basic stations | Low (Automated via Sawmill) |
| Metal Scraps | Floating debris, Merchant | Advanced tools, weapon parts | Medium |
| Ghost Goo | Defeated slimy ghosts | Recipe unlocks, advanced crafting | High (Combat required) |
| Blue Currency | Balloon's chests, Merchant kills | Merchant trading, high-tier items | Medium |

<Section icon={Target} title="Island Exploration & Merchant Cycles">

As you expand your reach, you will encounter various minor islands and a Wandering Merchant who visits your rig [svg4uQ6ZHYI]. The Merchant is an invaluable asset who stays for exactly one in-game day before departing [svg4uQ6ZHYI]. He accepts a special blue currency, which you can obtain by looting balloon chests floating in the sea or by defeating hostile tribe members [svg4uQ6ZHYI]. Knowing what to buy from the Merchant is key to maintaining your momentum [svg4uQ6ZHYI].

<Callout type="info" title="Merchant Schedule">
The Wandering Merchant remains at your rig for exactly 24 in-game hours (1 day). Prioritize buying rare resources or ammo bundles before his timer expires [svg4uQ6ZHYI].
</Callout>

When exploring minor islands, always keep an eye out for giant crabs and hidden chests [svg4uQ6ZHYI]. Crabs are an excellent source of raw food, while chests often contain vital fuel for your Dinghy or rare weapon scraps [svg4uQ6ZHYI]. Be cautious, as hostiles on these islands will occasionally attempt to steal your hard-earned resources if you leave them unattended [svg4uQ6ZHYI].

| Merchant Item | Best Use Case | Cost Efficiency | Recommendation |
| Ammo Bundles | Reloading Flintlock and rifles | Highly efficient | Highly Recommended |
| Metal Scraps | Fast-tracking advanced structures | Moderate efficiency | Recommended if short on time |
| Medkit | Instant healing during island raids | Highly efficient | Essential for hard fights |
| Wrench | Repairing structures | Poor efficiency | Not Recommended |
