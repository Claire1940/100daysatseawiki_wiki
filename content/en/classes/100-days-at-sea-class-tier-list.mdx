<Section icon={Target} title="Progression Path: How to Unlock New Classes">

Unlocking the top tiers of the **100 days at sea class tier list** requires a consistent flow of Pearls. Unlike Doubloons, which are lost upon death unless stored, Pearls are a persistent currency that stays with your account. Following a structured progression path will help you reach the 750-Pearl classes faster.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Resource Gathering">
    Start every run by using the harpoon to hook floating debris. Focus on surviving until Day 5 to earn your first set of progression rewards and basic badges.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Survivor Unlock">
    Redeem the active code **20Pearls** (valid as of June 2026) and combine it with Pearls found in island chests. Your first purchase should always be the **Survivor** class for 50 Pearls.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Island Hopping for Chests">
    Once stabilized, sail to nearby mysterious islands. Look for chests that contain both weapons and small amounts of Pearls. Avoid the Squid Boss until you have at least an A-Tier combat class.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Mid-Tier Investment">
    Save up 250-300 Pearls for the **Swordsman** or **Soldier**. This allows you to clear islands much faster, which in turn increases your Pearl-per-hour gain.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Endgame Specialization">
    Choose your final class based on your playstyle. If you play solo, go for **Raider**. If you play in a group, ensure one person has the **Medic** class (60 Pearls) to provide team-wide healing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Permanent Choices">
While you can switch between classes you have already purchased at the main menu, you cannot "refund" a class once bought. Choose wisely based on your long-term goals.
</Callout>

</Section>
