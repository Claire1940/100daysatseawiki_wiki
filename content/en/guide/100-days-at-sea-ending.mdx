<Section icon={Shield} title="Raft Base Fortification: Preparing for Day 100">

Your raft is your only sanctuary. To survive the final stages of the **100 days at sea ending**, your base must evolve from a simple platform into a floating fortress. The most objective measure of raft progress is the **Bonfire Level**. Upgrading your bonfire not only marks your progression but also unlocks the ability to sustain more complex structures and defenses.

By Day 90, you should aim for a "Bonfire Master" level of efficiency. Raids during the final ten days are relentless, often featuring multiple enemy types simultaneously. If your raft is too large, it becomes difficult to defend; if it is too small, you won't have room for essential utilities like the Cooking Pot and storage chests.

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Path to the Rescued! Badge">

Reaching the end of a 100-day run requires a disciplined approach. You cannot simply drift and hope for the best; you must follow a structured progression route that prioritizes safety over speed.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Resource Foundation (Days 1-20)">
    Focus exclusively on using the **Harpoon** to hook floating resources. Do not leave the raft for long trips. Build a basic perimeter and upgrade your Bonfire to at least level 3. Redeem the **20Pearls** code to buy a basic companion like a Turtle or Crab for extra health or damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Island Exploration & Weapon Procurement (Days 21-50)">
    Begin visiting mysterious islands to find chests. Your goal is to find a reliable gun (the **Firepower** badge unlock). Stockpile materials for the Cooking Pot. Ensure you have a class upgrade by Day 40—ideally moving from Sailor or Survivor to a combat-focused role like Swordsman or Sharpshooter.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Boss Conquest & Biome Navigation (Days 51-80)">
    Target the **Squid Boss** (Arms of Victory badge) and the **Ice Biome Boss** (King of the Snow). These encounters provide high-tier loot and the experience needed for the final stretch. Rescue penguins in the Ice Biome to secure secondary rewards.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Final Siege (Days 81-100)">
    Cease all non-essential exploration. Stay on the raft and focus on repairs and hunger management. Defend against the Ghost Galleon and Rival Rigs. On Day 100, the **Rescued!** badge will trigger, marking your official victory.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Final Push Tip">
During the last 20 days, the hunger meter drains faster. Ensure you have at least 20 bowls of Chowder stored in your chests to avoid having to fish during a night raid.
</Callout>

</Section>
