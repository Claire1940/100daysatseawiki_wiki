Your raft is your only sanctuary in the open ocean, and expanding it strategically is vital to surviving late-game raids [homepage-modules-2]. After collecting initial scrap with your harpoon, your first major task is to construct a grinder to convert debris into building materials [web]. Once your basic raft is established, prioritize utility structures like the Sawmill and the cooking pot over purely decorative expansions [web, homepage-modules-2]. A compact, functional raft is far easier to defend than a massive, unprotected platform [homepage-modules-2].

As your survival days accumulate, upgrading your bonfire becomes the most visible indicator of raft progression [homepage-modules-2]. Upgrading the bonfire expands your light radius, wards off cold, and unlocks advanced crafting blueprints [homepage-modules-2].
