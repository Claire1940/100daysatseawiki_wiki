### Core Survival Mechanics

Surviving the open ocean is not just about drifting; it is about establishing a functional base. The game's primary loop revolves around hooking floating debris with your harpoon, grinding down scrap, and upgrading your bonfire. The bonfire serves as your main progression marker, unlocking new recipes and structures as it levels up.

Additionally, managing your hunger and oxygen levels is critical. If you fall into the water without proper preparation, sharks and freezing temperatures will quickly deplete your health. By crafting a cooking pot early on, you can turn raw ingredients like fish and bird meat into nourishing stews that keep your character in peak condition.

<Callout type="tip" title="Grinder Priority">
Always prioritize feeding the grinder during the first three days. Unlocking advanced crafting recipes early on prevents you from getting bottlenecked when night raids begin.
</Callout>

### Volcano Region Walkthrough

To challenge the Lord of Lava, sail to the Volcano Region. You must locate and defeat three Fire Golems scattered across the surrounding volcanic islands. Pick up their defeated bodies and throw them into the mouth of the volcano to trigger an eruption. Jump into the caldera to face the Lord of Lava. Keep your distance and use ranged weapons to chip down his health while avoiding lava geysers. Defeating him rewards you with the powerful Magma Staff, which deals massive area-of-effect fire damage.

### Ice Biome Walkthrough

The Ice Biome requires a different approach. Your objective here is to rescue 20 penguins scattered across freezing islands. Watch out for hostile bears and wolves guarding the penguins. Once you collect all 20 penguins and return them to their mother at the center of the biome, the Yeti Boss will spawn.

The Yeti's deadliest attack is a ground stomp that can deplete half your health. When you see him wind up, use your harpoon to quickly pull yourself away to safety. Defeating the Yeti spawns a chest containing the Ice Mage tool, which allows you to build frozen platforms under your feet and shoot homing icicles at enemies.
