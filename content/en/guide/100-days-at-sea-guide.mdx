<Section icon={Shield} title="Raft Construction: Building the Ultimate Base">

Your raft is your only safe haven in the open ocean. A poorly planned raft will quickly crumble during nighttime raids, while a well-structured base provides the utility and defense needed to sustain long-term survival.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Hook Drifting Resources First">
    Before moving your raft or exploring, stand at the edge and use your harpoon to hook drifting wood, plastic, and metal. Stockpile these core materials in chest storage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Expand the Foundation Strategically">
    Expand your raft to at least a 6x6 grid. Keep vital structures like chests, the cooking pot, and your bed in the center, away from the outer edges where enemies attack.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Upgrade Your Bonfire">
    The bonfire is the heart of your raft. Upgrade it consistently from Level 1 to Level 9. Higher levels unlock advanced structures and keep you warm in cold biomes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Set Up Food and Healing Stations">
    Craft a cooking pot to prepare Chowder. Having a dedicated cooking area ensures you can quickly restore your hunger and health before embarking on long voyages.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Fortify the Perimeter">
    Install defensive walls around your core structures. Create clear combat lanes so you can shoot or strike attacking enemies without blocking your own movement.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Defense Note">
Nighttime raids scale in difficulty as the days progress. Never leave your raft completely undefended. If you are playing in a co-op group, ensure at least one player stays back to guard the base during island expeditions.
</Callout>

When targeting the **Squid Boss**, construct a raised platform on your raft. This allows your ranged attackers (such as Sharpshooters or Pirates) to fire safely from above while melee players manage any smaller minions that spawn during the encounter.
