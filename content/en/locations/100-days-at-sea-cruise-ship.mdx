<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Survival Loop">

To reach the legendary Day 100 milestone, you must master the daily routine of a survivor. The **100 days at sea cruise ship** experience is divided into distinct phases: gathering, building, and exploring.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Resource Gathering">
    Immediately upon spawning, use your harpoon to grab floating crates and planks. Do not jump into the water unnecessarily, as sea threats can end a run early. Focus on gathering enough materials to expand your platform by at least four squares.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Bonfire and Cooking Setup">
    Place a bonfire as soon as possible. This serves as your primary light source and progression marker. Build a cooking pot to create Chowder, which is essential for maintaining your hunger and health levels during long voyages.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Class and Companion Unlocks">
    Once you have collected enough Pearls (via codes or gameplay), visit the shop to unlock a class. The **Survivor** or **Medic** classes are the best early-game investments. Following this, roll for a companion in the Fisherman's Chest to assist with resource collection.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Island Exploration">
    After your raft is stable and you have basic weapons, begin sailing toward mysterious islands. These locations contain high-tier loot and unique bosses like the Squid Boss, which are required for advanced badges.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Harpoon Mastery">
The harpoon isn't just for resources; you can use it to pull yourself toward islands or even snag distant loot crates that contain rare weapons. Always keep your harpoon equipped when sailing.
</Callout>
