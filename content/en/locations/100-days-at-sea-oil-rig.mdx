<Section icon={Shield} title="Base Construction and Bonfire Upgrades">

Your raft is your only sanctuary. Building a functional **100 days at sea oil rig** requires more than just laying down planks; you need a strategic layout that protects your bonfire—the heart of your progression. The bonfire levels serve as milestones for your base's power and utility, unlocking better crafting options and defensive capabilities.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Resource Hooking">
    Use your harpoon to grab every piece of floating debris within sight. Focus on gathering enough wood and scraps to double the size of your starting platform within the first 2 days.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Bonfire Foundation">
    Upgrade your bonfire to Level 1 immediately. This provides light and acts as a central hub for your base. By Day 10, aim for Level 3 to unlock basic defensive structures.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Cooking and Sustenance">
    Place a cooking pot near your bonfire. Use gathered ingredients to cook chowder. This is essential for long-term survival as raw food provides minimal hunger relief and no health regen.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Defensive Perimeter">
    As you reach the mid-game (Day 40+), build walls and spikes. Rival rigs and sea creatures will become more aggressive. A well-defended core ensures your bonfire stays lit during raids.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Storage and Organization">
    Don't leave resources on the floor. Build storage chests to organize materials by type. This prevents accidental loss during combat and helps you track what you need for the next upgrade.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Night Pressure">
The ocean becomes significantly more dangerous at night. Ensure your bonfire is always fueled before the sun sets, or you'll find yourself fighting in total darkness against strengthened foes.
</Callout>

| Bonfire Level | Requirement | Major Unlock |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Level 1** | Basic Materials | Initial light source and base hub |
| **Level 5** | Mid-tier Resources | Advanced defensive structures and walls |
| **Level 9** | Rare Island Loot | Maximum base utility and "Bonfire 9" badge |
| **Master** | Speed Challenge | Special cosmetic and "Bonfire Master" title |
