<Section icon={Target} title="Battle Tactics: Step-by-Step Kraken Strategy">

Defeating the **100 days at sea kraken** requires tactical precision. The fight is divided into distinct phases, each requiring you to balance repairing your raft, dodging massive sweep attacks, and dealing focused damage to the boss's vulnerable points.

If you survive long enough, you may encounter the Double Kraken Defense scenario. This rare event requires you to split your focus and utilize automated defenses to prevent your raft from being torn apart.

<Callout type="success" title="Double Kraken Defense Tip">
When facing two Krakens simultaneously, deploy decoy rafts or floating barriers. This distracts one boss while you focus all your fire damage on eliminating the weaker of the two.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Preparation Phase">
    Before the storm hits, anchor your raft near a shallow reef if possible to limit the directions tentacles can spawn. Ensure all auto-turrets are fully loaded with Fire Cannonballs and your inventory has at least three stacks of wood for emergency repairs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Tentacle Control Phase">
    When the tentacles emerge, do not attack the main head. Focus on clearing the tentacles closest to your raft's control center. Use the Magma Staff to apply burn damage to multiple tentacles at once, forcing them to retreat beneath the waves.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Dodge the Suction Attack">
    When the water around your raft begins to whirlpool, the boss is preparing its suction attack. Hold onto a mounted structure or use a grappling hook to secure yourself to the deck. Failing to do so will pull you into the water, resulting in rapid damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Expose the Main Eye">
    Once four tentacles are temporarily defeated, the main head will surface near your raft. This is your window to deal maximum damage. Aim all magic attacks and muskets directly at the glowing eye to score critical hits before it submerges again.
  </Step>
</Steps>

</Section>
