<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Guide to the Lord of Lava Boss">

Defeating the boss within the **100 days at sea volcano** region is a milestone that very few players achieve, according to recent badge statistics. The Lord of Lava requires a specific tactical approach that balances raft positioning with precise ranged attacks.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Preparation Phase">
    Before engaging, ensure your raft is equipped with at least Level 5 Bonfire progress. Stock up on Chowder for health regeneration and ensure your primary ranged weapon is at full durability.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locating the Boss">
    Navigate to the center of the Volcano biome. Look for the distinct Magma Golem spawns which indicate you are nearing the boss arena. Keep your raft at a safe distance to avoid splash damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Ranged Engagement">
    Use a Blunderbuss or Flintlock to chip away at the boss's health. If you are playing as a Sharpshooter, maintain your reload rhythm to maximize the 50% gun damage buff.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Managing Add-Spawns">
    The Lord of Lava will summon smaller Fire Golems. Switch to a melee weapon like the Machete or Claymore to clear these quickly before they damage your raft structures.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Loot Collection">
    Once defeated, use your harpoon to quickly collect the drops. The boss often yields high-value materials necessary for crafting the most advanced naval defenses.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Raft Building and Defense Strategies">

In the **100 days at sea volcano** update, your raft is not just a platform; it is your only sanctuary. The environmental hazards of the volcanic sea can degrade standard wooden structures faster than the temperate zones. Expansion should be handled carefully to avoid creating a target that is too large to defend during raids.

Prioritize building defensive walls and ensuring that your cooking pot is easily accessible. Hunger management is crucial when your health is constantly under threat from heat exhaustion.
