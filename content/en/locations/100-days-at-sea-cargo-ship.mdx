Building a stable base is a multi-stage process. You cannot simply build a massive platform immediately; you must balance size with defensive capabilities. Follow these steps to transform your starter raft into a high-functioning cargo vessel.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Resource Hoarding">
    Use the harpoon exclusively for the first 3 days. Do not sail far. Focus on collecting planks, scrap, and plastic to build your first storage containers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Bonfire Hub">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Food Security">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Structural Reinforcement">
    Expand the raft in a 5x5 grid. Place defensive structures on the perimeter to prevent sea creatures from damaging your core internal components.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Weapon Procurement">
    Locate chests on nearby islands or use Pearls to buy a starter weapon. A Flintlock or Machete is necessary for handling island-based enemies.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Advanced Navigation">
    Once your raft is stable, begin the route toward the Volcano or Ice Biomes. These areas contain the materials needed for Level 9 Bonfires.
  </Step>
</Steps>
