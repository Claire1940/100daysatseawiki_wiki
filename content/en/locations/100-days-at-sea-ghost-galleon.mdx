<Section icon={Target} title="How to Find the Ghost Galleon Blueprint">

This item allows players to construct the Ghost Galleon for their own fleet, providing unparalleled stealth and speed during night cycles. However, the blueprint is not a guaranteed drop. Based on community data from 2026, the drop rate for the blueprint remains approximately 5-10% per successful encounter. Players can increase these odds by utilizing luck-enhancing consumables or engaging the ship during a "Blood Moon" event.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Strategy and Ship Loadouts">

Engaging the **100 days at sea ghost galleon** requires a specialized loadout. Standard iron cannonballs deal reduced damage to spectral entities. Instead, players should focus on "Holy" or "Silver" damage types. If you haven't unlocked these yet, fire-based attacks are a secondary alternative, as the ghost ship's tattered sails are highly flammable once physicalized.

Your ship's positioning is crucial. The Ghost Galleon utilizes a "Ghost Fire" broadside that can pass through your ship's hull, damaging internal components and crew without leaving a hole. Maintaining a diagonal "raking" position is the best way to minimize damage.

| Component | Recommended Upgrade | Reason |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Cannons** | Silver-Plated Long Guns | Increased damage against undead and range |
| **Hull** | Reinforced Oak with Iron Bands | High durability to withstand spectral collisions |
| **Sails** | Storm-Weave Sails | Necessary for maneuvering in the heavy fog winds |
| **Crew** | Priest / Exorcist NPC | Provides a passive "Holy Aura" buff to all attacks |

<Callout type="success" title="Pro Combat Tip">
Aim for the Ghost Galleon's rudder first. Disabling its movement prevents it from performing the "Phasing Dash" move, which allows it to teleport behind your vessel for a devastating surprise attack.
</Callout>
