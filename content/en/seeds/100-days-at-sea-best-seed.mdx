To achieve a 100-day survival streak, you must follow a structured progression path. This "seed" strategy ensures you are never caught off guard by the escalating environmental threats.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Harpoon Phase (Day 1-3)">
    Immediately upon spawning, use your harpoon to collect every piece of floating debris. Do not leave your raft yet. Your goal is to gather enough wood and metal to expand your platform by at least 4x4 squares.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Bonfire Milestone (Day 4-7)">
    Construct and upgrade your Bonfire. This acts as your primary progression marker. Reaching **Bonfire Level 1** is essential for basic warmth and signaling, but aim for Level 3 before heading to the first island.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Island Reconnaissance (Day 8-15)">
    Sail toward the nearest "Mysterious Island." Keep your raft close to the shore for a quick escape. Focus on finding chests that contain **Guns** or **Medkits** to prepare for the first major raid.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Companion Integration (Day 16-25)">
    By now, you should have enough Pearls from codes or exploration to roll for a companion. Aim for a **Salamander** or **Octopus** to automate resource collection while you focus on building.
  </Step>
</Steps>
