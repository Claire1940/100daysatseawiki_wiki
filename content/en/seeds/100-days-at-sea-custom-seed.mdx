<Section icon={Shield} title="Raft Base Building and Defense">

Your raft is your only sanctuary. Even the best **100 days at sea custom seed** will fail if your base is poorly constructed. You must balance expansion with defensive integrity. A common mistake is building a raft that is too large to defend during a raid.

**Essential Build Priorities:**
- **Compact Core**: Keep your Bonfire, Cooking Pot, and Storage in a central, protected area.
- **Combat Lanes**: Leave clear paths so you can move quickly to attack enemies boarding from different sides.
- **Verticality**: Building upward can provide better vantage points for ranged weapons.
- **Defensive Structures**: Use walls and traps to slow down enemies while you reload your weapons.

| Structure | Purpose | Build Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Bonfire** | Main progression and light | **Essential (Day 1)** |
| **Cooking Pot** | Food and health recovery | **High (Day 2-3)** |
