<Section icon={Shield} title="100 Days At Sea Container Shelter Construction">

Building a **100 days at sea container shelter** is the most effective way to reach the 100-day milestone. Unlike a flat raft, a container-style shelter uses walls and roofs to create a defensible interior. This structure protects you from the elements and provides a safe zone during the increasingly difficult night raids. As you progress, you will need to upgrade your bonfire, which acts as the heart of your base.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure the Foundation">
    Expand your raft to at least a 5x5 grid. Use the harpoon to pull in enough wood to ensure you have a stable platform that won't be easily destroyed by sharks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Erect Vertical Barriers">
    Place walls around your central 3x3 area. This creates the "container" effect, shielding your bonfire and storage crates from ranged enemy attacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Install Utility Stations">
    Place your cooking pot and storage sacks inside the shelter. Ensure you have clear lanes of movement so you can defend the perimeter without getting stuck on furniture.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Upgrade the Bonfire">
    Focus your resources on reaching Bonfire Level 5 as quickly as possible. This unlocks better crafting recipes and increases the overall durability of your shelter.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Reinforce with Defenses">
    Add defensive structures and traps to the exterior of your container shelter. Use weapons found in chests to arm yourself for the inevitable boss encounters.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Raft Stability">
Avoid over-expanding your raft too early. A massive, unfortified raft is harder to defend than a compact, well-built **100 days at sea container shelter**. Focus on height and reinforcement before width.
</Callout>

The progression of your base is tied to specific badges. For instance, the "Bonfire Master" badge is awarded to those who reach max bonfire levels efficiently. By Day 50, your shelter should be a multi-room fortress capable of sustaining long-term exploration missions to the Volcano or Ice biomes.
