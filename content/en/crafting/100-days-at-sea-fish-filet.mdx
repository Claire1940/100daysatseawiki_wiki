<Section icon={Target} title="How to Obtain and Process Fish Filets">

Acquiring the **100 days at sea fish filet** requires a mix of active gathering and strategic planning. The harpoon is your most versatile tool, allowing you to pull floating crates and aquatic life toward your raft without ever stepping into the dangerous deep. As you progress, you will transition from eating raw scraps to prepared meals that offer superior nutritional value.

  <Card title="Companion Assistance">

Once you have gathered your raw materials, the next step is processing. Using a Cooking Pot at your raft's bonfire allows you to transform basic filets into Chowder. This not only satisfies more hunger but also grants the "We Cookin" badge, a key milestone for any serious survivor.

<Callout type="warning" title="Night Raiders">
Food gathering becomes significantly harder at night due to increased enemy aggression. Ensure your raft is well-lit and your defenses are active before focusing on your harpoon.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Equip the Harpoon">
    Select the harpoon from your hotbar and aim at floating fish or resource crates in the water.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Hook and Retract">
    Click to fire the harpoon. Once it connects with a fish, it will automatically pull the item into your inventory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Ignite the Bonfire">
    Return to your raft and ensure your bonfire is active. You will need this to power the Cooking Pot.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Cook the Meal">
    Interact with the Cooking Pot to combine fish filets and other ingredients into Chowder for maximum efficiency.
  </Step>
</Steps>
